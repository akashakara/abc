KiarX Features And Services

Your search for the perfect software for your mining business ends here. Now let KiarX handle the heavy load of your work.

  • Live Mines Report

    KiarX provides you live production report of your mines with an in-depth analysis. See total trip count, production rate, production prediction, excavator loading rate, driver performance, truck performance and many more necessary data in a single place.

  • Garage Management

    With KiarX, manage maintenance and breakdown logs of your machinery via our app and website. Create new entries, check maintenance and breakdown history logs of any machinery and many more.

  • Diesel Management

    With KiarX, keep track of all your diesel related activities. Get the latest updates on total diesel consumption by each machine, available diesel in stock, diesel issued, and many more.

  • Inventory Management

    With KiarX, we are setting a new standard. Now manage your inventory with ease. Add or issue inventory stock, get low inventory stock real-time alerts and many more.

  • Live Location

    With KiarX, get to know your trucks, excavators, material-wise and dumping area live location. See current truck driver, current speed, loaded material and many more on the app and website.

  • Over Speed Alert

    Are you facing frequent accident incidents due to overspeeding of truck drivers? If yes then KiarX Intelligent AI is here which gives real-time notification in case any driver operates the truck beyond the speed limit.
